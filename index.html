OUR STORY

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Founded in Dubai in 2000, Chicking® has
grown from a single outlet into a global
brand with 400+ locations across 36+
countries

In India, we began on MG Road and now
operate 100+ outlets in cities like Kerala,
Chennai, Coimbatore, Bangalore,
Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Kashmir,
and Goa—delivering the perfect blend of
taste, quality, and affordability that families
love

OUR JOURNEY OF FLAVOR AND COMMITMENT

ChicKing® was founded with a vision to
bring happiness and great taste to
customers worldwide. Backed by India-
based Channel Foods Pvt. Ltd. and Mansor
Foods Pvt. Ltd., the brand began with a focus
on real, flavorful fried chicken.

Today, ChicKing® is a global favorite, known
for its quality, innovative menu, and
passionate growth—delighting customers
with over a decade of excellence.
